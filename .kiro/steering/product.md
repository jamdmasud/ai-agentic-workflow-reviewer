# Product Overview

## Agentic Workflow Reviewer

AI-powered multi-agent system for analyzing and optimizing DevOps workflows. Built for the Kiro Hackathon to demonstrate agentic AI patterns in workflow analysis.

### Core Purpose
- Analyze complex workflows (CI/CD, deployment, infrastructure) across multiple optimization goals
- Provide comprehensive risk analysis, bottleneck detection, and improvement suggestions
- Leverage specialized AI agents for different aspects of workflow analysis

### Key Features
- **Multi-format Support**: JSON, YAML, and plain text workflow inputs
- **Goal-based Optimization**: Reliability, Cost, and Simplicity optimization targets
- **Intelligent Caching**: Component-level caching with 2.8x performance improvements
- **Multi-agent Architecture**: Parser, Risk Analyzer, Optimization, and Critic agents
- **Interactive Analysis**: Real-time goal switching with optimized re-analysis

### Target Users
- DevOps Engineers optimizing CI/CD pipelines
- Platform Teams reviewing deployment workflows
- SRE Teams analyzing reliability patterns
- Development Teams seeking cost optimization

### Value Proposition
Transforms manual, time-consuming workflow reviews into automated, multi-perspective analysis with actionable recommendations and iterative refinement capabilities.
